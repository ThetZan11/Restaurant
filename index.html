Myanmar Tea Salad


Tea is a traditional Burmese food. Burmese people like to eat tea salad. Tea is always served at charity weddings and events. Tea salad mainly includes tea, beans, sesame seeds, tomatoes, dried shrimp, green chilies. Cabbage can also be added to the salad.
